Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Smart Parent Lock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of you and your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, addresses, or phone numbers from our users, including children under the age of 13.

App Permissions

The app requires specific permissions to function correctly as a parental control tool:

  • Device Admin (BIND_DEVICE_ADMIN): Used solely to prevent unauthorized uninstallation of the app by the child. We do not use this to wipe data or enforce password rules outside of the app's own lock screen.
  • Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Required to show the educational lock screen over other apps.
  • Usage Access (Optional): May be requested to detect which apps are running to trigger the lock screen.

2. Data Storage

All settings (PIN codes, challenge preferences, usage timers) are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this personal configuration data to any external server.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) and potentially other non-personal data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy

Since this app is intended for families and may be used by children, we configure ad requests to comply with Google Play's Family Policy. However, parents should supervise device usage.
